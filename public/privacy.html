1. Introduction

Welcome to Swift Typing ("we", "our", or "us"), operated by Suraj Maurya. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you visit swift-typing.me or use our application.

By using our website or app, you agree to the terms of this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, button clicks, and general navigation behavior (via Google Analytics).
  • Device & Browser Info: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution, and IP address (anonymized).
  • Local Storage Data: Your typing progress, settings, user profiles, and theme preference — stored locally on your device only. We do not transmit this to our servers.
  • Account Data (if using Supabase auth): Username and anonymized ID used to save your progress in the cloud.

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as your real name, phone number, or payment details directly.

3. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

  • Analytics: Google Analytics uses cookies to track usage patterns and help us improve the product.
  • Advertising: Google AdSense uses cookies to display relevant ads based on your interests and browsing history. These are third-party cookies controlled by Google.
  • Preferences: We store your theme preference (dark/light) in localStorage.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings or using the NAI opt-out tool.

4. Google AdSense & Advertising

Swift Typing uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites on the internet.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting aboutads.info.

For more information on how Google uses data when you use our site, visit: How Google uses data.

5. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and improve the Swift Typing service
  • To personalize your experience (e.g., remembering your theme and progress)
  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdSense
  • To analyze usage patterns and fix bugs
  • To communicate with you if you contact us via email

6. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Google Analytics — for usage statistics
  • Google AdSense / DoubleClick — for ad serving
  • Supabase — for cloud database services (arena match data, anonymous user IDs)
  • Netlify — our hosting provider

Each of these providers has their own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

7. Data Retention

Local typing progress and profiles are stored on your device indefinitely until you clear your browser data. Cloud arena match data stored in Supabase may be retained for analytics purposes. You may contact us to request deletion.

8. Children's Privacy

Swift Typing is intended for general audiences and is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability
  • Opt out of sale of personal data (we do not sell data)

To exercise these rights, contact us at surajmoriya200@gmail.com.

10. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted to our services.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance.
